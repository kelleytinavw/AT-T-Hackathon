Ask The TA


Ask The TA is an artificial intelligence (AI) incorporated narrow AI chat bot that answers questions regarding these topics:

Machine Learning
Augmented Reality
Internet of Things
Fifth Generation (5G)
Artificial Intelligence

The focus of this product is aimed towards informing everyday people about the different use cases that AT&T is solving with technology.
It is an interactive tool that teaches users how of these topics, in particular Artificial Intelligence can be utilized.

The streatch goal for this product is to have the AI chatbot work with a multitude of topics regarding
educating those who are curios about the STEM (science, technology and math fields).
